4 Card Content

The fields below allow you to edit your card's content.

{{element.name}}

1 Dimensions

Different networks have different card sizes. Choose the one you're sharing to

2 Template

Choose the template layout for your card.
Changing the template layout will delete any of your previously entered card content

3 Theme

Themes control the colour schemes for most of the cards. Choose the one you want to use

Scale ({{config.output.scale}}x) Choose the scaled output size

Scale controls the resolution of the final image. 1x will return the smallest image size. 2x will return an image of double scale, which is the default. 3x is useful if you need a very high resolution card. If you use 3x, please ensure the images you provide are also high resolution
